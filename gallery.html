Transformations

The landscaping of this home is clean, minimal, and well-maintained, complementing the house's inviting exterior. A neat row of trimmed boxwood shrubs lines the front façade, providing structure and symmetry beneath the front window. Bright yellow flowering plants add a pop of color near the walkway, creating a cheerful welcome to the entrance. Mulch beds help define the planting areas while adding contrast to the light-colored siding and stonework. The manicured lawn, though simple, keeps the focus on the crisp curb appeal and tidy presentation, making this home feel polished and cared for.

This property presents a classic blank canvas — a newly constructed home awaiting its finishing touch. With raw red clay covering the front yard and no existing greenery or pathways, the space is full of potential. The home's elegant brick and stonework facade, complete with dormer windows and a spacious front porch, provides a strong architectural foundation that's just waiting to be enhanced by thoughtful landscaping. From grading and soil preparation to planting and design, this is the perfect setup for a dramatic before-and-after transformation that will completely elevate the home's curb appeal.
