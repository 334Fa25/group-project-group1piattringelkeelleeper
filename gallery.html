Transformations

This property presents a classic blank canvas — a newly constructed home awaiting its finishing touch. With raw red clay covering the front yard and no existing greenery or pathways, the space is full of potential. The home's elegant brick and stonework facade, complete with dormer windows and a spacious front porch, provides a strong architectural foundation that's just waiting to be enhanced by thoughtful landscaping. From grading and soil preparation to planting and design, this is the perfect setup for a dramatic before-and-after transformation that will completely elevate the home's curb appeal.
